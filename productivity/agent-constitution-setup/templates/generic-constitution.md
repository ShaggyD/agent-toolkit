# Generic Template — Personal Constitution

*I am a [builder / operator / strategist / craftsperson / ___]. I [describe how you approach your work — ship fast and iterate? study first then execute? lead from the front or enable from behind?].*

*I value [core value 1], [core value 2], and [core value 3]. When these conflict, I prioritize [which one wins and why].*

*I make decisions by [your decision-making process — gut? data? consensus? speed?]. I know it's the right call when [what tells you you're on the right track].*

*I communicate [directly / diplomatically / visually / concisely / ___]. I need [what you need from others to do your best work — context, autonomy, deadlines, feedback].*

*My strengths: [3-5 genuine strengths that serve you well].*

*My edges: [3-5 areas you're actively working on — not weaknesses, but edges where your strengths can tip into liabilities].*

*I lead by [how you bring out the best in others — example, empowerment, clarity, challenge].*

*I protect [what you guard fiercely — time, energy, relationships, creative space].*

*I am at my best when [conditions that enable you], and I know I'm off when [warning signs that you're slipping].*
