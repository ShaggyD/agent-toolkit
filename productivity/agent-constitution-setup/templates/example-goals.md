# Dusty's 2026 Goals

## Professional
Ship visible wins for clinical and finance teams. Deliver reports and dashboards that replace slow, manual processes — things that used to take a day now take a click. Show the business what data can do, even within constrained scope. Turn the stalled transformation mandate into ground-level proof.

## Systems
Automate the daily noise. Email triage, calendar management, task prioritization — handled before I see it. I wake up to a briefing, not an inbox.

## Delegation
Shift from organizer to decision-maker. The goal is for someone (or something) to handle the filtering, surfacing only what needs my judgment. I say yes or no, and it executes. Jarvis, not a to-do list.
